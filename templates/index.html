About Electricity Bill Management System

The Electricity Bill Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the billing process for electricity services. It enables customers to easily view and manage their bills, make payments, and track usage.

For administrators and service providers, the system offers efficient tools to manage customer information, generate bills, and monitor electricity consumption. The system is secure, user-friendly, and reduces the complexity involved in traditional billing processes, providing a seamless experience for all users.
